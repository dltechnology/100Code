CASTLE FEAST

Fatten your royal. Raid theirs. Carry her home.

How to play
  • Move with the left stick. Attack and Interact with the right buttons.
  • Bring cake to your own Royal's cage and hold Interact to feed her — a fatter Royal is slower for enemies to carry off, buying your team time.
  • Break the enemy gate, break their Royal's cage lock, then walk into her to pick her up.
  • Carry her all the way back to your castle's return circle to win. You can't attack while carrying — get an escort!
  • Getting knocked out while carrying drops her on the spot.
